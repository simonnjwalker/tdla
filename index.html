About the TDLA

The Townsville District Law Association Incorporated (TDLA) (IA37315) is the association recognised by the Queensland Law Society as the official body representing the Townsville, Burdekin, Hinchinbrook and Goldfield regions.

The TDLA represents the interests of local lawyers with the Queensland Law Society and other organisations in relation to issues of concern for members.

The TDLA has over 300 members and represent most lawyers in the region
